About
Summary
J! Train is a web application that looks to improve skills in both recall and critical thinking. All Jeopardy! clues in shows since 2000 have been categorized and hints are available in the form of near-miss answers.
Usage Tips
  • Increasing the clue set is only recommended without category filters.
  • For clues that change with time (e.g. current president of country X), you can see the show's airdate by clicking "More Info".
  • Avoid simultaneous filters on all three criteria (category, round, and show type) as there may not be any results.
  • Hints can be turned off by setting hints to 1 in Advanced Settings.
